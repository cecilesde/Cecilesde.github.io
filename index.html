About

As a sports lover and MSc student in Biomedical Engineering at Imperial College London, with a MEng in General Engineering from Centrale Lyon, I build computational tools that interface deeply with human health. I’ve had the opportunity to work across both rigorous academic research and a leading MedTech company, which helped me seamlessly connect algorithmic logic to complex, real-world biological datasets.

I naturally thrive when driving team execution forward and bridging communication gaps—whether anchoring large-scale hardware testing validations or contextualizing complex machine learning architectures into meaningful clinical narratives. My ultimate goal is to leverage AI and deep tech to support individuals facing illness or injury to rediscover their potential, receive the high-fidelity care they need, and experience the profound joy of being active.
